Welcome to AI4Autism

This website collects all the information related to AI applied in autism that I know during my doctoral studies.

Aims

    Artificial intelligence (AI) and autism are my master's and doctoral research directions respectively. When I was a second-year master, I found that the probability of mental illness among my classmates was much higher than other diseases. Since then, I have developed a strong curiosity about neuroscience. Can AI help humans diagnose mental illness faster? Or can AI speed up the process of mental illness from diagnosis to recovery? Therefore, I created this website to collect all the information about AI and autism that I know during my PhD. By this website, I wish to raise public awareness of autism and stimulate ideas for using AI to enhance the lives of people with autism.
